Schedule (Markdown)

Supported formats:
Table: | 09:00 | 15 min | Session Name | (column order auto-detected)
Split timing: | 09:00 | 10+2 min | Session Name | (10 min talk + 2 min Q&A)
List: - 09:00 | 15 min | Session Name or - 09:00, 15 min, Session Name
Headings and blank lines are ignored. You can use ## Section headings to organize.
